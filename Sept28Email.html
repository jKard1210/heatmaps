The next step on our mission to bridge the information gap between retail investors and Wall Street

INTRODUCING

Alternative data has exploded in the world of professional investing over the last decade, as hedge funds and other institutions rush to gain an information edge over the rest of the market.

 

However, alternative data has been almost completely inaccessible to non-professional investors, with most providers charging thousands of dollars per year for access to their services.

 

Our goal is to bridge this information gap between retail investors and Wall Street, by making high-quality alternative data accessible to everyone.

 

The first step was collecting and aggregating dozens of alternative datasets (covering topics from U.S. Senators' personal stock trading to social media trends) on a free, easy-to-use website.

Since we launched the site in February, we've grown to over 22,000 registered users, and had over 200,000 unique visitors to our data dashboards last month.

 

We are excited to announce the next step of our journey, an alternative data API that will allow you to directly integrate Quiver's datasets into your own projects. This will make it easier for users with programming experience to do in-depth research and backtesting on our datasets, and execute trading strategies based on their daily updates. It will also allow us to explore powerful new partnership opportunities with brokerages and other platforms, and bring our alternative data to a wider audience.
